Curriculum Themes

The curriculum was designed to be inclusive and accessible to all participants, regardless of their prior experience with programming or neuroscience. The curriculum was divided into four main themes, each of which included a series of lectures, discussions, and hands-on activities. The themes were:

Introduction to Neuroscience and Neuroimaging
Python Programming Essentials
Network Analysis and Machine Learning
Neuroethics and Bias in Research
Open Science in Africa
